About Me

I am a Quality Assurance Engineer with hands-on experience in delivering high-quality, production-ready software through a strong blend of manual testing, Selenium WebDriver automation, and comprehensive API validation. My journey into quality engineering is rooted in a solid Computer Science , which enables me to approach testing with both functional accuracy and a security-first mindset.

Over the course of my professional experience, I have worked extensively on enterprise-grade applications including CRM systems, document verification platforms, and multi-currency financial remittance solutions. I have been responsible for end-to-end testing activities such as functional testing, regression testing, smoke and sanity testing, cross-browser validation, and User Acceptance Testing (UAT) across complex, multi-module architectures.

I actively design and maintain Selenium WebDriver automation scripts for critical business workflows, helping reduce repetitive manual efforts and improve overall regression efficiency. In addition, I perform in-depth API testing using Postman, covering request/response validation, authentication, negative scenarios, and third-party integrations to ensure system reliability.

My expertise also includes SQL-based database validation, where I verify transactional integrity, backend data consistency, audit trails, and automated communication workflows. I collaborate closely with developers, product owners, and international clients in Agile/Scrum environments, contributing to root cause analysis, defect management, UAT sign-offs, and production release support to deliver stable, scalable, and business-aligned software solutions.
